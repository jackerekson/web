About My Neighborhood

I live in the BEAUTIFUL Bellevue Wisconsin, a village next to Green Bay Wisconsin. If you like green area this is the place to be. It rains enough that everything is green, there is tons of lakes and we have the Great Lakes right next to us giving us the feel of living on the ocean without the tourists everywhere! Lots of good food but even better lots of fishing!
